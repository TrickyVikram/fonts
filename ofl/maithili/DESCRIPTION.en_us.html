Maithili is a traditional typeface designed specifically for the Maithili language, spoken in Bihar, Jharkhand, and parts of Nepal. Based on the Devanagari script, this font preserves the cultural heritage and linguistic identity of the Maithili speaking community.

The design incorporates traditional letterforms with modern readability, making it suitable for both digital and print applications. The font supports all essential Maithili characters, conjuncts, and diacritical marks required for proper text rendering.

Key features include: - Complete Devanagari character set for Maithili - Traditional letterform design - Optimized spacing and kerning - Support for complex conjuncts - Clear distinction between similar characters

This font aims to promote digital literacy in Maithili and preserve the language's written form for future generations.
